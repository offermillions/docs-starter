---
title: "Autorole: automatically assign roles to new members"
description: "Automatically assign up to 5 roles to members when they join your server. Roles are only assigned to humans — bots are skipped automatically."
---

Autorole automatically assigns one or more roles to every human member who joins your server. This is useful for granting a default member role, access to channels, or any other role you want new members to have from the moment they arrive.

<Note>
  All autorole commands require the **Manage Roles** permission.
</Note>

## Add a role

```text
,autorole add (role)
```

You can add up to 5 roles to the autorole list. When a new member joins, fault assigns all configured roles at once.

```text
,autorole add @Member
,autorole add @Newcomer
```

**Role hierarchy requirements:**

- The role must be below the bot's highest role in the role list.
- The role must be below your own highest role (unless you are the server owner).

<Warning>
  If a role is above fault's highest role, the bot cannot assign it and will return an error. Drag the **fault** role above any autoroles in **Server Settings → Roles**.
</Warning>

## Remove a role

```text
,autorole remove (role)
```

Removes a role from the autorole list. Members who already received the role are not affected.

```text
,autorole remove @Newcomer
```

## List configured autoroles

```text
,autorole list
```

Shows all roles currently configured for autorole.

## Clear all autoroles

```text
,autorole clear
```

Removes all roles from the autorole list. Existing members are not affected.

## Notes

- **Bots are skipped.** When a bot joins your server, fault does not assign autoroles to it — only human members receive them.
- **Alias:** `ar` — for example, `,ar add @Member`.
